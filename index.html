Worth-while Macho Temple

Explore the 7-storied Macho Temple in San Fernando City, La Union it is blisfully standing on a hill. This taoist temple was dedicated to Mazu, a Chinese sea-goddess. The must-see here are the Liang Thing Pagoda, the Bell Tower and the Drum Tower, the Majestic Five Door Gate, the Bamboo Garden and the Golden emblem of the dragon.

Take a Deep Breath at San Juan

La Union is known for its great waves at Urbiztondo Beach that the tourists actually want when they want to surf. Surfing in La Union reportedly started back in the 70's when a Japanese trained the locals to surf. Surfing in La Union Urbiztondo Beach costs Php 400 per hour with instructor and surf board and Php 500 for half day lesson (surf board rental is separate charge). It costs Php 200 for surf board rental per hour.
